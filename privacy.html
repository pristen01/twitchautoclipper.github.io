Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TwitchAutoClipper ("the Application", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use TwitchAutoClipper, we may collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Data: When you authorize the Application with your TikTok account, we access your TikTok Open ID, display name, and account information as permitted by the TikTok API scopes you authorize (including user.info.basic and video.upload).
  • Twitch Account Data: Your Twitch username and stream metadata required to monitor streams and generate clips.
  • YouTube Account Data: OAuth credentials to upload video content to your YouTube channel via the YouTube Data API.
  • Video Content: Short video clips (90–180 seconds) recorded from Twitch live streams for the purpose of posting to TikTok and YouTube Shorts.
  • Configuration Data: Your preferences such as streamer lists, posting schedules, and sensitivity settings stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide the core functionality of TwitchAutoClipper:

  • To authenticate with TikTok, Twitch, and YouTube APIs on your behalf
  • To monitor Twitch streams and detect high-engagement moments
  • To upload video clips to your TikTok account using the TikTok Content Posting API
  • To upload video clips to your YouTube channel using the YouTube Data API
  • To track upload history and daily posting statistics locally
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or TikTok data with any third parties. All data is used exclusively for operating the Application on your behalf.

3. TikTok Data Usage

TwitchAutoClipper uses the TikTok Content Posting API. In relation to TikTok data specifically:

  • We access your TikTok Open ID and basic profile information to verify authorization
  • We use your authorization token to upload video content to TikTok on your behalf
  • We do not access your TikTok followers, messages, or private data
  • We do not store TikTok access tokens beyond what is necessary for the Application to function
  • TikTok data is never transferred to advertising networks or data brokers
  • You can revoke TikTok access at any time via TikTok Settings → Security → Authorized Apps

4. Data Storage

TwitchAutoClipper stores data locally on your own device:

  • A local SQLite database tracking clip history and upload records
  • OAuth tokens stored in a local credentials folder on your machine
  • Temporary video files deleted after upload (unless configured otherwise)

We do not operate cloud servers that store your personal data. All processing happens on your local machine.

5. Data Retention

Configuration and upload history data is retained locally until you delete it. OAuth tokens are refreshed automatically and can be deleted by removing the credentials folder. Temporary video clips are deleted after successful upload by default.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • API credentials are stored locally and never transmitted to our servers
  • We recommend storing your .env file securely and never sharing it
  • All communication with third-party APIs uses HTTPS encryption

7. Third-Party Services

TwitchAutoClipper integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Twitch: https://www.twitch.tv/p/legal/privacy-notice/
  • YouTube/Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the Application's access to your TikTok, Twitch, or YouTube accounts at any time
  • Delete all locally stored data by removing the Application and its data folder
  • Request information about what data is stored locally by inspecting the Application's database file

9. Children's Privacy

TwitchAutoClipper is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please visit our main website.
