Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use TwitchAutoClipper, we authenticate you via Twitch. We only access the data required to run the clipping service, which may include your Twitch username, ID, and stream clip data based on the scopes you authorize.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used strictly to provide the core functionality of TwitchAutoClipper (generating and managing clips on your behalf). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Retention and Security

We do not permanently store your Twitch viewing history. Authentication tokens are handled securely and used only to communicate with the Twitch API.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our main website.
